About the Playbook

Better Data. Better Health.

The purpose of this Playbook is to capture the learnings of the MITRE team as they, with the support of a growing community, launched the CodeX HL7 FHIR Accelerator, developed the minimal Common Oncology Data Elements (mCODE) and other FHIR Implementation Guides, and deployed FHIR-based solutions to help patient, providers, and others in the real world. It is hoped that MITRE's experience can provide a recipe to successfully launch and implement new healthcare interoperability initiatives.

Organization

MITRE Corporation

MITRE was established to advance national security in new ways and serve the public interest as an independent adviser. We continue to deliver on that promise every day, applying our systems-thinking approach to provide solutions that enhance our national security and way of life.
